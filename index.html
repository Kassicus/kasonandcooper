Our Story

Kason and Cooper met through a pair of mutual friends (Erin and Eric) and a misscommunication on April 29th 2022 at Coopers Brothers Highschool Jazz Band concert. Shortly after this night Eric, Erin and Kason all became roomates. Cooper began to spend a lot of time around their apartment.


The two became friends almost immediately and started growing closer quickly. Around mid June of 2022 they went on their first date where Cooper cheated at mini golf.


In May of 2023 the pair decided they were going to move in together. They wanted to live together before comitting to taking the relationship further. They moved into their first apartment in June and both found this to be a massively positive move in their lives and it only cemented their goals for the future.


Now the couple is excited to enjoy your company at their wedding as they prepare to start the rest of their lives together.

The Ceremony will begin at 4:00pm. Guest arrival is 3:30pm.



The centerpiece at each table is a cake. These cakes are the dessert bar, after we cut the wedding cake we invite our guests to mings and try cakes at each of the tables.



Guests will be invited during the dinner hour to toast or roast the couple.
